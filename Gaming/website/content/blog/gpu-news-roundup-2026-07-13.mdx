1. **Vertex Shading**: The GPU processes vertex data, including positions, normals, and texture coordinates.
2. **Geometry Shading**: The GPU processes geometric data, including primitives and transformations.
3. **Pixel Shading**: The GPU processes pixel data, including colors, textures, and lighting.

* **VRAM Usage**: The amount of video memory used by the GPU.
* **CPU/GPU Bottlenecks**: The amount of data that can be transferred between the CPU and GPU.

To mitigate these bottlenecks, developers can use several techniques, including:

* **Texture Compression**: Compressing textures to reduce VRAM usage.
